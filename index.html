Weekly Picks — March 30, 2026

Six Shows Worth
Your Saturday Night

01
Musical Staff Pick

The Rocky Horror Show

Revival · Cult Classic · Interactive

An absolute blast and genuinely the most fun you can have in a darkened room with strangers. Bring a bag of rice. Wear something weird. Leave your dignity at the door — you'll want to.

🗓 Running Now ⏱ 2h no intermission ★★★★★
02
Shakespeare Intense

Titus Andronicus

Classic · Bloody · Essential

Shakespeare's most deranged bloodbath — and somehow one of his most riveting. The current production commits fully and doesn't look away. Not for the faint-hearted, but deeply worth it.

🗓 Running Now ⏱ 3h with intermission ★★★★★
03
Musical Charming

The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee

Comedy · Musical · Audience Participation

Absolutely disarming. Pulls you in laughing and then — without warning — punches you right in the chest. The audience participation section alone is worth the ticket price.

🗓 Running Now ⏱ 1h 45m ★★★★★
04
Musical Dark Comedy

Heathers The Musical

Cult · Teen Angst · Gloriously Unhinged

How very. The songs are catchier than they have any right to be, the staging is bold, and the whole thing swings between hilarious and genuinely unsettling in the best possible way.

🗓 Running Now ⏱ 2h 15m ★★★★★
05
Solo Show Essential

Every Brilliant Thing

Drama · Intimate · Life-Changing

One performer. The entire audience. A list of everything worth living for. You will laugh more than you expect, cry more than you're prepared for, and leave utterly changed. Go alone. Or don't.

🗓 Running Now ⏱ 70 min no intermission ★★★★★
06
Musical Spectacle Hot Ticket

Cats: The Jellicle Ball

Reimagined · Voguing · Extraordinary

The revival nobody asked for but absolutely everyone needs. Ballroom culture meets Lloyd Webber in a fever dream that somehow makes Cats make sense for the first time. Revelatory.

🗓 Running Now ⏱ 2h 30m ★★★★★
